Dollhouse Domesticity

by Erica David

This misguided dollhouse pushes the limits of our comfort within the domestic space. By replacing the traditional domestic setting of a dollhouse with a strange, almost inhospitable environment, domestic objects find new homes and uses within this altered space. In response, the play happening within the space no longer resembles traditional domestic play, moving beyond the instilling of capitalist and domestic ideals from a young age and allowing an expansion of the ideas of ‘normal’ in the setting of the domestic.
